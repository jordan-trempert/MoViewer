Privacy Policy for MoViewer

Last updated: 9/5/2024

Introduction

Welcome to MoViewer, developed by Stardom Studios. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how MoViewer handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information or data from users. MoViewer is designed to function entirely on your device without transmitting any data to our servers or third-party services.

Data Storage

All data related to your use of MoViewer, including your preferences, settings, and any content you manage within the app, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor is it shared or uploaded anywhere outside of your device.

Security

Although we do not collect or store your data, it is your responsibility to ensure that your device and the information stored within MoViewer are kept secure. We recommend using appropriate device security measures such as passcodes or encryption to protect your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

From time to time, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. If we make any changes, the updated Privacy Policy will be available on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
